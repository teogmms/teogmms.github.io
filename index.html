I am a third year PhD student at Laboratoire d'Analyse et de Mathématiques Appliquées at Université Paris-Est Créteil and at the Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona, under the supervision of Stéphane Sabourau and Florent Balacheff.

My research interests lie in the domain of Riemannian geometry, often from a metric geometric point of view. Recently, I have been interested in scalar curvature, in the topology of manifolds with positive scalar curvature and in metric geometrical generalisations of scalar curvature lower bounds. I have also worked on systolic geometry, geometric inequalities and Finsler geometry.
